TruckAppA TruckersMP companion app

The power under the bonnet.

Fancy a listen to the graceful throbbing of the V8's pistons?
Well, we can't offer you that in our app, but here's what you may find handy on your virtual trips across Europe:

Server list

A cursory look will reveal how many players are currently online on the game's servers.

Meetup list

Take part in player convoys – see where and when they take place & RSVP in a few taps.

Meetup reminders

You'll never forget to show up at a convoy – just let your phone remind you. Easy, innit?

Not quite enough? We agree!
Since this is an early alpha version, new features are being added as we go.

We have many exciting ideas for the app and we're waiting for yours, too! Take a look at what's up and coming.

Choose your flavour!

Petrol or diesel? That's always the question.
